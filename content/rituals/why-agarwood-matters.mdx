Agarwood is not a trend note; it is **depth training**. The material asks you to approach with patience — heat, fraction, listening.

A keeper box is not packaging snobbery. It is a small vault for something that improves when respected.

To burn it well is to refuse the cheap thrill. The reward is quieter: a room that remembers you differently.

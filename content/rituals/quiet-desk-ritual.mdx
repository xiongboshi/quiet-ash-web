A quiet desk is edited the way a poem is: what remains must earn its weight. Tray for edges, sleeve for ash, bowl for the pause between paragraphs.

The ritual is tactile before it is spiritual — **grain under the wrist**, paper squared, breath matched to a thin ribbon of smoke.

If the desk is honest, it will not look like a shop display. It will look like work in progress, held by a few good objects.
